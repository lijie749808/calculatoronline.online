# Privacy Policy for CalculatorOnline

## Introduction

Welcome to CalculatorOnline, your comprehensive **online calculator platform** offering 200+ specialized calculators for mathematics, finance, health, and daily calculations. At CalculatorOnline, your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our calculator services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using CalculatorOnline:

1. **Account Information**

   - **What We Collect**: This includes your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account (if applicable).
   - **Purpose**: To manage your account and provide customer support.

2. **Usage Details**

   - **What We Collect**: Information about how you use CalculatorOnline, including which calculators you access, calculation frequency, and usage patterns.
   - **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement, improve our calculator tools, and add new calculators based on user needs.

3. **Device Information**

   - **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access CalculatorOnline, such as device type, operating system, and browser type.
   - **Purpose**: To optimize our calculator tools for different devices and ensure compatibility across mobile and desktop platforms.

4. **Cookies**

   - **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences and improve user experience.
   - **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our calculator services and personalize your calculation experience.

5. **Calculation Data**
   - **What We Collect**: We do not store your personal calculation inputs or results. All calculations are performed locally in your browser.
   - **Purpose**: Anonymous usage statistics only to improve our calculator services.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. The minimal data we collect is stored securely on our servers, and we implement a variety of security measures including encryption and access controls to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.
- To provide services through trusted third-party partners who assist us in operating our website or conducting our business, subject to confidentiality agreements.
